My First Post

In those final weeks, I knew better than to walk near my mother. Most of the time, she just looked at me with the stink-eye, but sometimes, without warning, she would grab me by my throat and hang on, until I pried her fingers from me. She didn't bother talking to me unless it was to make death threats. "When you grow up, you'll meet me in a dark alley when you least expect it, and then I'll do away with you, and nobody will ever know." Literally, gloating as she said this...
